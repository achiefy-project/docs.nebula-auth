# Account groups

![](../../.gitbook/assets/mw-groups.png)

Groups let you organize accounts by purpose and work with them faster in the list.

Using groups you can:

* filter accounts
* perform bulk actions
* find the accounts you need faster

One account can belong to only one group.

***

### Adding an account to a group

**Option 1: Through the context menu**

1. Right-click the account
2. Select **Groups → Create group**
3. Enter the group name

***

**Option 2: Through the "Group" field**

1. Select the account
2. Enter the group name in the **"Group"** field
3. Press **Enter**

If a group with that name already exists, the account will be added to it.

***

### Removing an account from a group

To remove an account from a group:

1. Right-click the account
2. Select **Remove from group**

***

### How to delete a group

A group exists as long as it contains at least one account.

To delete a group, remove all accounts from it. After that, the group will disappear automatically.

***

### Storage

The group is stored inside the **maFile**, just like the proxy.

This means that when you move the file between different NebulaAuth instances, the group is preserved with the account.
