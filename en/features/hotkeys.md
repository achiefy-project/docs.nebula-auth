# Hotkeys

Some hotkeys are available in NebulaAuth to speed up work.

At the moment they are not configurable.

***

#### Globally in the window

* `Ctrl+V` — paste a maFile from the clipboard
* `Ctrl+F` — focus the search field
* `Esc` in search — remove focus from the search field

***

#### In the account list

Works on the selected account

* `Ctrl+C` — copy login
* `Ctrl+X` — copy maFile
* `Ctrl+Shift+C` — copy the saved password, if available

***

#### In the groups field

* `Enter` — add the selected account to the entered group

***

#### In the proxy list

* `Delete` — remove the proxy from the selected account

***

#### In modal windows

In most windows:

* `Esc` — close the window
* `Enter` — confirm the action
